Your goal is to survive the journey to Organ in about 50 in‑game days. Every day you will make choices, face random events, and try to keep your family alive. This is a text‑input game, so you will type commands to play.

    CHOOSE YOUR CLASS
    Before the journey begins, you pick one of three classes. Each one changes how your game plays out.

Medic:

    Better at treating sickness and injuries

    Higher chance to save family members

    Not very good at hunting

Hunter:

    Very good at hunting and can bring back lots of food

    Higher chance of eating poisonous plants by mistake

    Strong food production, risky survival

Farmer:

    Can identify safe plants and forage without getting poisoned

    Higher chance of dying from medical problems

    Good for steady food, weaker in emergencies

    DAILY SURVIVAL
    Each day you will face events like weather, sickness, broken wagon parts, hunting opportunities, and random dangers. Your choices affect your health, food, supplies, and how many days you can keep going.

    TEXT COMMANDS
    Type commands to take actions. Common commands include:

travel  - move forward on the trail
hunt    - try to gather food
forage  - search for plants
rest    - recover health
status  - check your supplies, health, and days left
help    - show available commands
quit    - end the game

    MANAGE YOUR RESOURCES
    You must keep track of food, health, medicine, wagon durability, and the number of days remaining. Running out of any of these can end your journey early.

    REACH ORGAN
    If you survive long enough and make it to the end of the trail, you win. If not, the trail claims another traveler.
